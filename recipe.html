Chocolate Cake Recipe

"Here is a fast and easy version of the Chocolate Cake Recipe. Enjoy!!!"

Difficulty Preparation Cooking Total Time
Easy 10 minutes 25 minutes 35 minutes

Ingredients

Black chocolate: 200g

Sugar: 150g

Butter: 200g

Eggs: 4

Flour: 80g

Instructions

1

 Melt the chocolate and the butter together

2

 Mix the sugar, eggs and the flour

3

 Mix everything to get a smooth dough

4

 Bake in the oven
